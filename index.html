ABOUT

My name is Térence, I'm 23 and I come from the region of Charleroi. I started to code 3 years ago while I was studying advertising at HELHa Mons and I really fell in love with it. That's where it all started...

After 2 years in advertising studies I decided to dedicate myself entirely to code and went on with a UX Webdesign training at Technocité. But I felt I needed more. Therefore, I began another training at BeCode Charleroi in order to become a Web Developer.

As for now, I'd say I'm front-end oriented. I'm feeling comfortable with HTML5, SASS, JavaScript & the use of Git/GitHub and I'm currently learning React.js and WordPress to upgrade my skills. However, I'm really not chilly about learning new techs in front-end as well as in back-end.

My purpose is to find an internship to continue learning and get a real experience to start my career.
